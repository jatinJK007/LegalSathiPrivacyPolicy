02Nature of the Service & Legal Disclaimer

LegalSaathi provides general legal information derived from publicly available Indian statutes (such as the Indian Penal Code, Consumer Protection Act, Rent Control legislation, and the Information Technology Act). It does not provide legal advice, does not create an attorney-client relationship, and is not a substitute for consultation with a licensed advocate. Outputs generated by the App may be incomplete, outdated, or inapplicable to your specific circumstances. You should independently verify any information before relying on it and consult a qualified lawyer for matters requiring legal representation or case-specific advice.

04How We Use Information

  • To process your queries and generate informational responses grounded in the App's legal reference material.
  • To maintain your local chat history and app preferences for continuity between sessions.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues if and when you voluntarily report them (e.g. via support email).
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

06Data Storage, Retention & Deletion

  • On-device storage: Chat history and preferences are stored in a local database on your device and persist until you clear them via Settings → Clear History, or until you uninstall the App.
  • Bundled legal corpus: The reference legal text and associated vector embeddings used to ground responses are bundled with the App at install time and do not contain any user data.
  • Server-side retention: The current version of LegalSaathi (V1) does not operate a backend server and does not persist your queries on infrastructure controlled by us. Query text is transmitted directly to Google's Gemini API for processing; retention on Google's side is governed by Google's own data retention policies.
  • Deletion: You may delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing app data/cache through Android system settings or by uninstalling the App.

07Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the data processed, including transmission of query data over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections to third-party APIs. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08International Data Transfers

Because query text is processed by Google's Gemini API, your data may be transmitted to and processed on servers located outside India, including in jurisdictions with different data protection standards. By using the App, you acknowledge this cross-border transfer for the purpose of generating responses to your queries.

09Children's Privacy

LegalSaathi is not directed at children under the age of 18 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10Your Rights

If you are located in India, you may have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), including the right to access, correct, and request erasure of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. If you are located in the European Economic Area, you may have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR); if located in California, you may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA. Because most data relevant to your use of LegalSaathi is stored locally on your own device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by clearing app data or uninstalling the App. For data processed by third parties (Section 5), please refer to their respective rights-request mechanisms, or contact us and we will assist where feasible.
